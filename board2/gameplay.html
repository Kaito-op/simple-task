Game Description

Snake and Ladder is a classic board game where players race to reach the final square (100) by rolling dice. The game board has 100 squares arranged in a 10x10 grid, with snakes and ladders placed on various squares.

Objective: The goal is to be the first player to reach square 100. You move according to the roll of the dice. If you land on a square with the base of a ladder, you climb up to a higher square. If you land on a square with the head of a snake, you slide down to the tail of the snake.

Rules

  • Players take turns rolling a single dice.
  • The number rolled determines how many squares the player moves forward.
  • If a player lands on the base of a ladder, they move up to the top of the ladder.
  • If a player lands on the head of a snake, they slide down to the tail of the snake.
  • The first player to reach or exceed square 100 wins the game.
  • If a player rolls a number that would take them past square 100, they do not move and their turn ends.
